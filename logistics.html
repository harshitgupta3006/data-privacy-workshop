Logistics


Travel

Participants will be paid a travel reimbursement of up to 5000 INR. A higher reimbursement might be available to those coming from far off places. One must participate on all days of the event to be considered for travel reimbursement. The exact reimbursement amount will be announced after the event.



Accommodation

The participants will be provided on-campus accommodation as per availability .


IIT Delhi Campus



The nearest metro stations to IIT campus are IIT Delhi Metro Station and R.K. Puram Metro Station (both on Magenta line). Here are the ways in which you can reach IIT Delhi.

From New Delhi Railway Station (distance 15 km): Book a cab from pre-paid (taxi / auto-rickshaw) booths outside the station. If you prefer metro, the nearest metro station (IIT Delhi Metro Station) is within walking distance.

From Airport (distance 12 km): You can take a cab to reach IIT Delhi. Alternatively, from Terminal 1, you can take magenta line to IIT Delhi or R.K. Puram metro station.
