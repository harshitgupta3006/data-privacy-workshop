IIT CS Workshop

The CS Theory group is excited to host a four-day winter school, supported by Mohit Aron endowment, intended to introduce participants to interesting research areas of Theoretical Computer Science. This school will be held from 11th December, 2023 to 14th December, 2023 at IIT Delhi campus. The school will have lectures by various faculty members on topics ranging from Algorithms, Computational social choice, Communication complexity, Security protocol verification and more. There will also be tutorials, activity sessions, opportunities for informal interaction with the faculty members and graduate students at IIT Delhi.
